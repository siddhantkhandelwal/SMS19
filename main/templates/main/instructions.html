  • Click on the menu button on the top left corner of the screen to navigate to the different sections (Market, Portfolio, Leaderboard and News).
  • Your current balance in Indian rupees is displayed at the top of the screen.
  • You can purchase desired number of units of a particular stock at the Market by selecting the stock and then clicking buy.
  • You can purchase stocks from Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), New York Market (NYM) and Japan Stock Exchange (JPN), keeping in mind the respective currencies used.
  • The Portfolio section shows the stocks you have purchased and here is where you can either buy more units of the stock(s) you have or sell them.
  • You can view your rank in the Leaderboard section.
  • Check the News section periodically for updates and maximise profits. 3 news articles will be added every 10 hours.
